Jaime Woo

Jaime is an award-nominated writer who focuses on the intersection of culture and technology, and co-publisher of the incident response zine "The Post-Incident Review." He could eat dumplings until he bursts. He lives in Toronto, and has a dog named Taco.

How to Write for Ourselves and Publish for Others

Everyone has a story to tell. In this session, I share my writing process, and why I believe that everyone can write. We'll go through brainstorming ideas, refining them, transforming them into words, and then polishing them until they sparkle. Then, we'll discuss how to approach publishing, and sharing our stories with others.
