Building a Smart Mirror

Smart Mirrors are mirrors with a display behind the reflective surface. They look fancy and futuristic, but they are actually quite simple to build! I built my own and conducted a study on how Smart Mirrors may be used day-to-day. I will demonstrate how I built and how I use my smart mirror!
