Chalk The Talk: How Sketchnoting Makes You A Better Speaker!

Sketchnoting is a form of visual note-taking that has become a powerfull tool for learning, story-telling and recalling complex concepts in tech. In this talk, you'll learn what sketchnoting is (with examples) and understand how you can get started (with tips & resources). Most importantly, you'll walk away understanding why getting creative with your note-taking can have great impact on your public speaking, personal brand and professional growth.
