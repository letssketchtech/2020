Frequently Asked Questions

Will this be recorded?

We are working on it! We'll announce details about that soon.

Do you still need speakers?

We are at capacity for speakers. We're floored that so many talented people want to participate, but we can't make this conference any longer!

Some of the talks are in the middle of the night for me. Should I still buy a ticket?

We know that the timing doesn't work for every timezone, but we don't expect anyone to sit at their computer for 12 hours anyway! This event is designed for you to be able to drop in for as much as you want. We'll do our best to make slides available afterwards. Also, see question 1!

Do I need any special equipment or tools?

All you need is pen and paper! More information will be posted shortly about every workshop. No specialized equipment (e.g. iPads, tablets, Procreate, etc.) is required.

Can my children/family attend with me?

Yes! You don't need to buy a separate ticket for your kids. Kids ages 9 and up will get the most out of this, but all ages are welcome. Some talks will be angled towards technology professionals.

Do you need sponsors?

We are looking for sponsors. Send us a DM on twitter to get a copy of our sponsorship prospectus.
